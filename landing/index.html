A local-first thinking system.

Not notes. Not tasks. Not productivity.

A calm instrument for unfinished thinking.

What Strata is for

Strata is where thinking goes when it won't settle.

Some thoughts loop. Some decisions sit unresolved for days. Some ideas aren't ready to be acted on, filed, or forgotten. They just need somewhere to exist without pressure.

Strata gives you a place to write those things down — not to store them, but to stop carrying them. Each entry is named by what kind of thinking it is, not where it belongs. You're not organizing information. You're observing the shape of what's on your mind.

The point isn't to remember. It's to let go.

Strata is

  • for unresolved thoughts
  • for ideas that aren't ready
  • for questions without answers
  • for decisions that need to be set down
  • for observing mental structure

Strata is not

  • a notes app
  • a task manager
  • a daily journal
  • a productivity dashboard
  • a cloud service

Structure, not features

Strata uses six cognitive structures. These are not categories or tags. They name the state of your thinking.

Thought

Something surfacing. Not yet shaped, not yet directed. Present, but unnamed until now.

Idea

Something with potential. It may lead somewhere, or it may not. It exists to be held, not executed.

Question

Something unresolved. Not a search query. A real question — the kind that stays with you.

Decision

Something closed. A choice has been made. Named so it can stop circling and be set down.

System

Something recurring. A pattern, a process, a loop. Named so it can be seen clearly.

Insight

Something that arrived. Not learned — realized. Worth writing down before it fades.

Thought Idea Question System Decision Insight

You choose a structure to name the state of your thinking. Strata reflects that back to you. Nothing is forced to become action.

Why local-first

Your data lives on your machine. The files are plain and readable. There are no accounts, no cloud sync, no external dependencies.

This means your thinking stays yours. It isn't processed, analyzed, or stored elsewhere. If you stop using Strata, your files remain exactly where they are, in a format you can open with anything.

No lock-in. No dependency. Just files on your computer.
